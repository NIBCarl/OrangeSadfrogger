ABOUT ORANGE SADFROGGER

Orange Sadfrogger $OSF is a meme coin inspired by one of the iconic Hedz characters, hand-drawn by Matt Furie, the creator of Pepe the Frog. It combines the nostalgic allure of Furie's artwork with the speculative energy of meme coins.

OSF celebrates the "sad but hopeful" vibe of its mascot, creating a tongue-in-cheek commentary on the ups and downs of the crypto world.

With a community-first approach and zero team token allocation, Orange Sadfrogger is here to bring joy, nostalgia, and hopefully some green candles to your portfolio.
